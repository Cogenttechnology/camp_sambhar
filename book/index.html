Four ways to feel Sambhar

01

Flamingo Bird Watching

Sunrise walks with local naturalists. Over 100 species recorded by the Rajasthan Forest Department.

02

Professional Stargazing

Telescopes, laser sky tours and astronomy stories under some of Rajasthan’s darkest skies.

03

Salt-Making Trail

Walk the working salt pans and see how salt has been harvested here for over a thousand years.

04

Sambhar Village Tour

Heritage lanes, ancient temples and the British-era narrow-gauge salt railway with a local guide.
