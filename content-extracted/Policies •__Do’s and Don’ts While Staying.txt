CAMP SAMBHAR RESORT
Do’s and Don’ts While Staying
No Smoking in and nearby the Tent –
A Big NO to Smoking in and nearby the Tents
Rather let your lungs inhale the fresh air.
Support our Local Staff –
Stand with us to Empower LOCALS
We feel pride to employ nearby villagers.
Let’s share the pride and be kind to them.
Don't Feed Our Pets –
We are Guarded by sincerest and playful German Shepherd DOGs
Our friendly DOGs are the sincerest guards.
They are open and playful.
If you prefer to keep distance with them, say NO or ask us to chain them.
3. Be Prepared for Possible Electricity Failures
Expect occasional power interruptions in our rural setting.
Rest assured, our backup generator ensures a continuous power supply during emergencies.
Your patience is appreciated.
4. Sambhar Brochure and Local Guidance
For local insights, request a Sambhar Brochure from the resort office.
Need in-person help? Visit our office, our staff is ready to assist.
6. Choose Your Dining Experience
In-Tent Dining: Order food to your tent using the provided number.
Restaurant Dining: Visit the restaurant for a sit-down meal in the designated area. Bon appétit!
