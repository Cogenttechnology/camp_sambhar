CAMP SAMBHAR RESORT
Privacy Policy
Camp Sambhar Resort values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information.
1. Information We Collect
We may collect the following information:
Name
Mobile number
Email address
Postal address
Booking details
Payment-related information (processed through secure payment providers)
Any information voluntarily submitted through enquiry forms or contact forms.
2. How We Use Your Information
We use your information to:
Process bookings and reservations.
Respond to enquiries.
Provide customer support.
Share booking confirmations and important updates.
Improve our website and guest experience.
Send promotional offers or newsletters (only where permitted).
3. Information Sharing
We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.
Your information may be shared only:
With trusted service providers involved in processing bookings or payments.
When required by applicable law or government authorities.
4. Cookies
Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to improve website performance, analyze visitor traffic, and enhance user experience.
You may disable cookies through your browser settings; however, certain website features may not function properly.
5. Data Security
We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.
6. Third-Party Services
Our website may integrate third-party services such as payment gateways, booking engines, Google Maps, or analytics tools. These services operate under their own privacy policies.
7. Your Rights
You may request to:
Access your personal information.
Correct inaccurate information.
Request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal or operational requirements.
8. Children's Privacy
Our website is not intended to collect personal information from children without the consent of a parent or guardian.
9. Policy Updates
This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. The latest version will always be available on this website.
