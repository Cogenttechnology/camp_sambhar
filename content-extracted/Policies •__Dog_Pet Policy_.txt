Dog/Pet Policy -
We have a pair of German Shepherd dogs at the property
Our GSDs are well-socialized and trained
Your dog will be your responsibility in case of any mishappening
Your dog must be fully vaccinated. Please carry its vaccination certificate.
Swiss cottage tents are sensitive and made up of wood and Canvas so any harm to the tents or the property will be borne by you.
We charge Rs 1500/- for the pet... coz the tent needs dry cleaning.
The dog needs to be taken out of the property strictly for the nature call.
The Dog must not be left unattended, neither inside the room nor outside.
