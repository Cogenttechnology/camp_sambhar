CAMP SAMBHAR RESORT
Sustainability & CSR
Travel with Purpose
At Camp Sambhar Resort, we believe that tourism should leave a positive impact on both nature and the local community. Nestled near the magnificent Sambhar Salt Lake, every experience we create is guided by respect for the environment, wildlife, and the people who call this region home.
We are committed to promoting sustainable tourism by encouraging responsible travel practices while preserving the unique ecosystem of India's largest inland salt lake.
Our Sustainability Initiatives
🌿 Protecting Nature
Promoting eco-friendly tourism around Sambhar Salt Lake.
Regular cleanliness and waste management drives within and around the resort.
Responsible waste segregation and disposal practices.
Reduced use of single-use plastics wherever possible.
Water conservation through mindful usage and efficient management.
Tree plantation and native plant conservation initiatives.
🦩 Conserving Wildlife
Supporting ethical birdwatching practices without disturbing migratory birds.
Educating guests about the biodiversity and ecological importance of Sambhar Lake.
Promoting responsible photography and wildlife observation.
Raising awareness about wetland conservation through guided experiences.
💧 Resource Conservation
Energy-efficient lighting across the property.
Encouraging guests to reuse towels and linens to reduce water consumption.
Responsible use of natural resources throughout resort operations.
Supporting the Local Community
We believe that sustainable tourism should also create opportunities for local people.
Our efforts include:
Employing local residents and supporting regional livelihoods.
Partnering with local guides, artisans, and service providers.
Promoting local cuisine, traditions, and cultural experiences.
Encouraging guests to explore nearby villages respectfully and responsibly.
Guest Responsibility
We invite every guest to become a part of our sustainability journey by:
Respecting wildlife and natural habitats.
Avoiding littering around the lake and surrounding villages.
Conserving water and electricity during their stay.
Choosing responsible tourism practices.
Supporting local communities through ethical travel.
Our CSR Commitment
Corporate Social Responsibility is an integral part of Camp Sambhar Resort's vision.
Our CSR initiatives focus on:
Environmental conservation and wetland protection.
Nature awareness and educational programs for schools and colleges.
Community engagement and local livelihood support.
Plantation and cleanliness campaigns.
Promoting sustainable tourism in the Sambhar region.
Every stay at Camp Sambhar Resort contributes to our mission of preserving Sambhar Lake's natural heritage while creating meaningful experiences for future generations.
Together, let's protect nature, celebrate local culture, and travel responsibly.
